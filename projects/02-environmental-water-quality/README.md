# Environmental Water-Quality Laboratory Experience

## Case Summary

During my internship at the Department of Environment and Forestry of the Bangka Belitung Islands Province, I supported environmental laboratory analysis and water-quality monitoring.

I processed and analyzed more than 20 environmental water samples per week while following laboratory procedures, quality standards, documentation requirements, and team workflows.

## Responsibilities

- Supported environmental water-sample handling
- Performed microbiological testing, including coliform analysis
- Tested chemical parameters such as total nitrogen and total phosphate
- Operated UV-VIS spectrophotometry equipment
- Operated Atomic Absorption Spectrophotometry equipment
- Maintained laboratory records
- Supported quality-control processes
- Contributed to environmental monitoring and reporting

## Remote-Work Transferable Skills

Although laboratory testing is normally conducted on site, the experience developed capabilities that are directly useful in remote work:

- Structured data recording
- Quality-control review
- Spreadsheet tracking
- SOP compliance
- Documentation accuracy
- Scientific reporting
- Error identification
- Team communication
- Environmental-data interpretation
